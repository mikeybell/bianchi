WRINKLELESS MOULDING

Wrinkles in the carbon material affect the property of the whole frame and add more weight. Bianchi avoid wrinkles by pre-tuning the thickness of the laminate of each frame. Increased frame and process efficiency. Reduced weight.

BAT K-VID

Technology and the Kevlar Vibration Isolating Device allows us to obtain different stiffness behaviors under different forces directions, without wasting power transfer. Special design of the rear rectangle. Kevlar insertions in the seat stays and the carbon fork. Improved shock absorption and adherence on the road.

DISC BRAKE SYSTEM

Bianchi is one of the first bicycle companies in the world to introduce hydraulic disc brakes in its Road carbon range. Infinito CV features a disc specific model with rear triangle and fork redesigned by Bianchi to accomodate the disc brake system. For 2015, Bianchi introduces the new Impulso aluminum frame with disc brake system and significantly reduced frame weight.
